Shipping & Returns

Last updated: October 21, 2025

We’re committed to delivering your stories safely and efficiently. Below you'll find details about our shipping timelines, delivery options, and return policies.

1. Shipping Times

Orders are typically processed within 2–3 business days. Shipping times vary based on your location and selected delivery method:

  • Standard Shipping: 5–7 business days
  • Expedited Shipping: 2–3 business days
  • International Shipping: 7–21 business days

Note: Delivery times may be affected by holidays, customs delays, or carrier disruptions.

2. Shipping Rates

Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on your location and selected shipping method. Free shipping is available for domestic orders over $75.

3. Order Tracking

Once your order ships, you’ll receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can also log into your account to view order status and tracking updates.

4. Returns & Exchanges

We want you to love your experience. If you're not satisfied, you may return eligible items within 30 days of delivery:

  • Items must be unused and in original packaging
  • Digital products are non-refundable once accessed
  • Return shipping costs are the responsibility of the customer unless the item was defective or incorrect

To initiate a return, please contact myuntoldstory.contact@gmail.com with your order number and reason for return.

5. Damaged or Incorrect Items

If your order arrives damaged or incorrect, please notify us within 7 days of delivery. We’ll make it right by offering a replacement or refund.

6. International Orders

International customers are responsible for any customs duties, taxes, or import fees. We are not liable for delays caused by customs processing.

If you have questions about shipping or returns, please reach out to myuntoldstory.contact@gmail.com. We're here to help.
