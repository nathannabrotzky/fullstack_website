Our Story

My Untold Stories is a creative studio dedicated to crafting immersive experiences through fictional and historical storytelling. We blend digital artifacts, physical products, and narrative design to spark curiosity and wonder.

Our Mission

To inspire imagination and connection through timeless stories, beautifully designed products, and accessible digital experiences. We believe every story deserves to be told — and every user deserves to feel included.

How It All Began

Founded by a team of designers, developers, and storytellers, My Untold Stories began as a passion project to reimagine how people engage with history and fiction. What started as a single letter from a fictional explorer has grown into a full-fledged experience platform.

Our Core Values

  • Empathy

    We design with compassion, ensuring accessibility and inclusion in every experience.

  • Craft

    We obsess over details — from pixel-perfect layouts to historically accurate artifacts.

  • Curiosity

    We celebrate exploration, mystery, and the joy of discovering something new.

  • Integrity

    We build with transparency, respect, and a commitment to ethical storytelling.
